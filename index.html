Moves. Controls. Things.

Flexible

Create systems to control nearly anything. Routers. Lathes. Milling machines. Laser cutters. 3D printers. Even robots and quadcopters!

Universal

Machinekit works on many devices. Use Beaglebone Black, Raspberry Pi, ARM devices, or the spare PC in your closet. Run the GUI from the host or a remote tablet.

Reliable

Realtime Linux threads with Xenomai, RTAI, or RT_Preempt threads, and non-real time with vanilla Posix threads for simulation and testing.
